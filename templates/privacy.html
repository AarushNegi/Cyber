Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

ZeroKey ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. What We Collect

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Username and full name
  • Mobile number and date of birth (for account verification)
  • Email address (when signing in via Google)
  • Profile picture (provided by Google OAuth, if used)
  • Cryptographic public keys for FIDO2 passkey authentication
  • Login timestamps and IP addresses for security purposes

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We never store your password in plain text — all passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • We never store biometric data (fingerprints, face scans) — these stay on your device
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never display advertisements

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your identity when you sign in
  • To maintain your account and session security
  • To detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • To improve the security and reliability of the service

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — for "Sign in with Google". Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • MongoDB Atlas — for secure cloud database storage
  • Render.com — for application hosting

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database) with encryption at rest
  • All communication is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens are stored in HTTP-only, Secure cookies
  • FIDO2 public keys are stored — private keys never leave your device

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: contact@zerokey.dev

This privacy policy applies to the ZeroKey authentication system hosted at cybersec-ax8r.onrender.com. This is a research and development project.
